# payroll-app
Double Sided ISCM LLC — Payroll App
The Double Sided ISCM LLC Payroll App is a lightweight, mobile-friendly web application built specifically to manage weekly employee wages and time tracking for our business. It runs entirely in the browser and is accessible directly from the iPhone home screen, functioning just like a native app without requiring any downloads from the App Store.
The app allows managers to track clock-in and clock-out times for each employee across every day of the work week. Break durations are entered in minutes and are automatically deducted from gross hours before any calculation is made. The system calculates net hours, regular pay, and overtime in real time — any hours exceeding 8 per day or 40 per week are automatically billed at the standard 1.5× overtime rate, with visual warnings displayed the moment an employee enters overtime territory.
Each employee has their own dedicated card showing their name, role, and hourly rate, all of which can be edited directly within the app. Managers can toggle individual work days on or off, add weekly notes for remarks such as sick days, bonuses, or deductions, and clear or copy schedules with a single tap. A shift template system allows commonly used shifts to be saved and applied instantly, eliminating repetitive data entry across the week.
The weekly payroll summary at the bottom of the screen displays a full breakdown per employee including regular hours, overtime hours, break time deducted, and total pay owed. A monthly overview panel provides a bird's-eye view of every week in the month, showing total payroll figures at a glance and allowing quick navigation between weeks. A live sticky bar pinned to the bottom of the screen always shows the running weekly total, net hours, overtime hours, and total staff count as figures are entered.
All data is saved automatically to the device using browser localStorage, meaning no internet connection is required after the initial load and no data is ever sent to an external server. Payroll records can be exported at any time as a CSV file and shared directly via the iPhone Share Sheet to email, AirDrop, or any other app, providing a clean paper trail for record keeping.
The app is hosted on GitHub Pages at a permanent URL, ensuring it is always accessible from any device through Safari and can be updated instantly by uploading a new version to the repository. It supports both dark and light display modes, responds naturally to left and right swipe gestures for week navigation, and is fully optimized for iPhone screens including safe area handling for the notch and home indicator.
In summary, this app replaces manual spreadsheets with a fast, reliable, and purpose-built payroll tool designed around the daily workflow of Double Sided ISCM LLC — simple enough to use in seconds, but comprehensive enough to handle the full complexity of weekly wage calculation with overtime, breaks, and multi-employee management.
